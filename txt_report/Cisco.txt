Cisco
Sustainability Goals
2020 Sustainability Goals
Avoid 1 million metric tons of GHG emissions within the supply chain between FY12 and FY20 (achieved in 2019)

2022 Sustainability Goals
Reduce total Scope 1 and 2 GHG emissions worldwide by 60% absolute 
Use electricity generated from renewable sources for at least 85% of global electricity 

2025 Sustainability Goals
Reduce foam packaging use by 75%, as measured by weight 
Improve product packaging efficiency by 50%, as measured by package volume per weight of product 
70% of its manufacturing suppliers by spend will achieve a “zero waste” diversion rate at one or more site(s)
Decrease use of virgin plastics by 20%
80% of component, manufacturing, and logistics suppliers by spend will have a public, absolute GHG emissions reduction target 

2030 Sustainability Goals
Reduce supply chain-related Scope 3 GHG emissions by 30% absolute

Latest Sustainability Report Highlights
Achieved 55% absolute reduction in total Scope 1 and 2 greenhouse gas emissions in 2020 compared to a 2007 baseline
83% of total global electricity was generated by wind, solar, and hydroelectric sources, including 100% in the U.S. and parts of Europe, and 60% in India in 2020
33% of suppliers reported a public, absolute GHG emissions reduction target in 2020; 10-point increase compared to the previous year
Reduced the use of virgin plastic by 26.1% in 2020 compared to 2018 baseline 
Reduced foam used in packaging by 11.5% in 2020 compared to a 2019 baseline
40% of its executive leadership team are women 
Donated $53 million in cash and personal protective equipment to nonprofit organizations and first responders during COVID-19
81% of employees participated in community impact programs in 2020
Positively impacted 527 million people through social impact grants and signature programs
Recognized as World’s #1 Best Workplace in 2019 and 2020 by Great Place to Work®
18 facilities, representing 13% of its global real estate portfolio, were LEED-certified, with 13 rated Gold or Platinum
Purchased 100% renewable electricity for facilities in the U.S., Belgium, Denmark, France, Germany, Ireland, Italy, Switzerland, and the United Kingdom
Diverted approximately 80% of waste generated at facilities from landfills globally
Launched its first products with closed-loop plastic in 2020
